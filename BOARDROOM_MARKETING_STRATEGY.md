### JON LOOMER — The Technical Blueprint

> "A lot of people think you need $500 to test Facebook ads. You don't. You need a **structured test with clear hypotheses.** Here is exactly how I would spend the first $50."

**The $50 Test Structure (Day 1–5)**

Create ONE campaign. **Objective: Leads** (not sales — get the email first, sell in the sequence).

| Ad Set | Audience | Daily Budget | Days | Total |
|--------|----------|-------------|------|-------|
| Ad Set 1 | Nigeria · 22–35 · Interests: Freelancing, side hustle | $2/day | 5 | $10 |
| Ad Set 2 | Nigeria · 22–35 · Interests: Web design, graphic design | $2/day | 5 | $10 |
| Ad Set 3 | Nigeria · 22–35 · Interests: Entrepreneurship, make money | $2/day | 5 | $10 |
| Ad Set 4 | Nigeria · 22–35 · Interests: Tech, software, AI | $2/day | 5 | $10 |
| Ad Set 5 | Nigeria · 22–35 · Broad (no interest targeting) | $2/day | 5 | $10 |
| **Total** | | | | **$50** |

**The Ad Creative (same creative for all 5 ad sets):**

- Format: 30-second vertical video
- You on camera, screen recording overlay
- Hook (first 3 seconds): *"This AI tool found 47 businesses in Lagos with no website in 8 minutes."*
- Body: Show the Runvax search happening in real time
- CTA: *"I'm teaching this in a free training. Link in bio."*
- Lead to: Free training opt-in page

**After 5 days, evaluate:**

| Metric | Kill if | Keep if |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Cost per lead | Above ₦3,000 | Below ₦1,500 |
| Click-through rate | Below 1% | Above 2.5% |
| Relevance score | Below 4 | Above 6 |

Kill the 3 worst ad sets. Take remaining $350 and put it into the 2 winners. **That is how $50 becomes profitable data and $350 becomes your scale budget.**

**Pixel Setup (Do this before spending $1):**

1. Install Meta Pixel on your landing page
2. Create custom audiences:
   - "Visited landing page but didn't opt in" (retarget with testimonial ad)
   - "Opted in but didn't buy" (retarget with urgency ad)
   - "Bought course" (exclude from ads, add to email sequence)
3. These audiences cost nothing to build. They are worth everything at scale.

## DAVID OGILVY — THE COPY PRINCIPLES

> "I insist on five principles before a single word of copy is approved:"

**1. Research the customer obsessively.**
Before writing any ad for Runvax, interview 10 web designers in Lagos. Ask them:
- "What is your biggest problem getting clients right now?"
- "What have you tried that didn't work?"
- "What would your life look like if you had 5 clients every month?"
- "What do you worry about at 2am?"

Their answers are your copy. Word for word.

**2. The headline is 80% of the ad.**
Five times as many people read the headline as the body copy. If your headline doesn't sell, the rest doesn't matter. Test at least 5 headlines for every campaign.

**3. Long copy works — if it earns every word.**
For a ₦65,000 purchase, people need convincing. A 1,000-word sales page will outperform a 200-word one if every sentence answers an objection or builds desire. Do not be afraid of length. Be afraid of boring.

**4. The testimonial is the most powerful element on any page.**
One video of a real person saying "I made ₦180,000 in my first month" is worth 10 paragraphs of your copy. Lead with social proof. End with social proof. Everything else is filler.

**5. Never run an ad you would be ashamed for your grandmother to see.**
The Nigerian market has been abused by "get rich quick" schemes. Runvax is real. The results are real. Your copy must be honest and specific. The moment it crosses into hype, you lose the intelligent buyer — and in Nigeria, the intelligent buyer talks to everyone they know.
