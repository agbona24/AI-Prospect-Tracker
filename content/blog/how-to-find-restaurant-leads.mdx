Restaurants are one of the easiest verticals to prospect in. They're visual businesses — a website with good food photos and a menu converts hungry visitors into customers — yet a huge share of independent restaurants and eateries still don't have one.

That gap is your opportunity.

---

## Why Restaurants Are a Strong Niche

Restaurants make ideal early clients because:

- **They understand the value of visuals.** You don't have to sell them on "why a website matters" — they already know good photos and menus drive walk-ins.
- **They're easy to find in bulk.** Every city has hundreds of them, searchable by neighborhood.
- **The ask is concrete.** "A website with your menu, hours, and location" is a five-second pitch, not an abstract service.
- **Add-on revenue is built in.** Online ordering integration, Google Maps optimization, and social media links are natural upsells after the first sale.

---

## Step 1: Search Restaurants by City

Open [ProspectAI](https://prospectai.beamai.net) and search "restaurants" in the city you want to target. You'll get a list of every listed restaurant, café, and eatery — each flagged for whether it currently has a website.

Narrow by neighborhood if the city is large; restaurants in busy commercial districts tend to have higher revenue and a bigger budget for a website.

---

## Step 2: Prioritize the No-Website Restaurants

Focus first on restaurants with no website. They're your warmest leads — actively losing customers to competitors who show up in Google search and Maps with photos, menus, and reviews front and center.

ProspectAI flags each result automatically, so you can filter straight to the businesses most likely to say yes.

---

## Step 3: Pitch With Photos, Not Features

Restaurant owners respond to *what it will look like*, not technical specs. Skip words like "responsive design" or "CMS integration." Say this instead:

> **Subject: A quick mockup for [Restaurant Name]**
>
> Hi [Owner Name],
>
> I noticed [Restaurant Name] doesn't have a website yet — and you're missing out on customers who search Google before deciding where to eat.
>
> I design simple, mobile-friendly restaurant websites with your menu, photos, hours, and location — live within a week.
>
> Want to see a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI generates a personalized version of this email for every restaurant in your results.

---

## Step 4: Follow Up With a Visual

If you have 15 minutes, mock up a simple homepage with the restaurant's name and a stock photo before following up. A visual follow-up converts far better than a second cold email — restaurant owners are visual decision-makers.

---

## Step 5: Price for Recurring Revenue

Restaurants are a great fit for ongoing plans, not just one-off builds:

| Package | Includes |
|---|---|
| Starter site | Menu, hours, location, contact form |
| Standard | + online ordering link, photo gallery, Google Maps embed |
| Managed | + monthly menu updates, social media posting, review monitoring |

The managed tier turns a single restaurant client into recurring monthly revenue — and restaurants change menus and promotions often enough to justify it.
