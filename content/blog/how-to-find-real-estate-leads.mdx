Real estate agents live and die by lead generation — yet a surprising number of independent agents and small agencies still rely entirely on listing portals and social media, with no website of their own to capture leads directly.

---

## Why Real Estate Is a High-Value Niche

- **They already understand lead generation.** Agents are sold on the value of leads every day — a website that captures buyer/seller inquiries is an easy sell.
- **Listings are natural content.** A property listing page is content the agent already has (photos, descriptions, prices) — you're just packaging it well.
- **Commission-based buyers.** Agents earning commission on high-value sales are comfortable spending on tools that bring in even one extra deal.
- **Referral potential.** Real estate agents know other agents — a strong result with one client often leads to referrals within their network.

## Step 2: Prioritize Independent Agents With No Website

An agent relying solely on a listing portal profile or Instagram has no way to capture direct leads or build a personal brand — a gap you can point to directly.

---

## Step 3: Pitch Around Lead Capture, Not Just Presence

Real estate pitches convert best when they focus on lead ownership:

> **Subject: Are you capturing your own leads, [Agent Name]?**
>
> Hi [Agent Name],
>
> I noticed you don't have your own website — which means every lead from your listings goes through a portal you don't control.
>
> I build simple real estate websites with your listings, a lead capture form, and your personal brand — so buyers and sellers can reach you directly.
>
> Want to see a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI generates a personalized version of this outreach for every agent in your results.

## Step 4: Design for Listings and Lead Capture

When you follow up with a mockup, prioritize:

- A clean listings grid with photos, price, and key details
- A prominent lead capture form ("Get notified about new listings")
- Agent bio and contact information above the fold
- Mobile-friendly design — most property searches start on a phone
