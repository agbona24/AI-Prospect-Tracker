Kano is the commercial capital of Northern Nigeria and home to one of the largest trading economies in West Africa — yet it has almost no dedicated web design market. If you're a designer willing to work there, you're arriving early to a city with real money and almost no local competition.

Kano's business culture rewards trust and formality over flashy pitches. Get the approach right and you'll find a market that's more loyal, and often more lucrative, than flashier southern cities.

## Top Industries in Kano With Untapped Website Demand

### 1. Textile and Leather Export Companies
Kano has been West Africa's textile and leather trading hub for generations, with Kantin Kwari market alone moving enormous volumes of fabric. Many exporters trade internationally by phone and WhatsApp alone — no catalogue site, no way for a buyer abroad to browse stock before calling.

**Why they're ideal clients:**
- Export buyers in Ghana, Niger, and beyond need a way to view stock remotely
- A simple catalogue site adds instant credibility for cross-border deals
- These businesses have real cash flow and understand paying for value

**Typical project value**: ₦150,000 – ₦400,000

---

### 2. Agricultural Trading and Processing Companies
Kano is a major hub for grain, groundnut, and produce trading, plus a growing base of food processing companies. Most operate with no web presence despite dealing in large volumes.

**Why they're ideal clients:**
- B2B buyers (processors, distributors) expect to verify a supplier online before committing
- A basic company profile site signals legitimacy for larger contracts
- Government and NGO agricultural programmes often require an online presence to partner

**Typical project value**: ₦180,000 – ₦450,000

---

### 3. Private Hospitals and Specialist Clinics
Kano's healthcare sector has grown significantly, with private hospitals and specialist clinics serving both the city and a wide catchment across the North. Most rely on signage and reputation alone.

**Why they're ideal clients:**
- Patients and referring doctors search online increasingly, even in Kano
- A clear services and contact page reduces the burden on phone lines
- Owners are typically well-educated professionals who respond to formal proposals

**Typical project value**: ₦200,000 – ₦450,000

---

### 4. Government Contractors and Manufacturing Companies
Bompai's industrial estate is home to manufacturers and government-facing contractors — construction firms, equipment suppliers, and industrial services companies — many of which have never had a website.

**Why they're ideal clients:**
- Public sector tenders increasingly require some form of verifiable online presence
- Manufacturing companies with real production capacity can afford proper corporate sites
- These are long-term, referral-rich clients once trust is established

**Typical project value**: ₦300,000 – ₦800,000

---

### 5. Islamic Schools and Private Academies
Kano has a large and growing private education sector, including Islamic academies and international schools serving a wealthier, education-focused population.

**Why they're ideal clients:**
- Parents compare schools before enrolling, especially for secondary and international options
- Proprietors make quick decisions
- Termly content updates create a natural retainer relationship

**Typical project value**: ₦150,000 – ₦350,000

## The Kano Cold Outreach Approach

Kano business culture leans formal, respectful, and relationship-first. A pitch that works in Lagos can land as pushy in Kano. Lead with respect and clarity, not urgency.

**Message template:**

> Assalamu alaikum [Name], my name is [Your Name], a web designer based in [City]. I came across [Business Name] while researching [industry] businesses in Kano, and noticed you don't currently have a website. A professional website could help you reach new customers and buyers beyond your existing network. I would be glad to prepare a free sample concept for your review, with no obligation. Please let me know if this would be of interest.

WhatsApp is the dominant channel for business communication across Northern Nigeria — lead with WhatsApp over email where you have a number, and always follow up rather than sending one message and waiting. ## Pricing in Kano (2026)

Kano pricing sits below Lagos and Abuja but above smaller cities like Jos — trading and manufacturing wealth in the city supports solid mid-tier pricing once trust is built.

| Package | Price | Scope |
|---------|-------|-------|
| Starter | ₦80,000 – ₦150,000 | 5 pages, mobile, WhatsApp button |
| Business | ₦180,000 – ₦400,000 | 8–10 pages, catalogue/gallery, basic SEO |
| Corporate / Export catalogue | ₦300,000 – ₦700,000 | Multi-language option, product catalogue |
| Monthly maintenance | ₦15,000 – ₦40,000/month | Ongoing updates |

Never lead with your lowest price in Kano — trading communities respect confident, fair pricing far more than an immediate discount, which can read as a lack of seriousness.
