## Top Industries to Target in Port Harcourt

### 1. Oil and Gas Service Companies
The energy sector in Rivers State is supported by thousands of small and medium service companies: drilling support, logistics, safety consulting, environmental services, marine services, recruitment.

These companies regularly work with Shell, TotalEnergies, Chevron, and Agip — and a professional website is often a **prerequisite** for vendor registration.

**What they need:**
- Corporate website with company profile, services, and certifications
- Team and leadership pages
- Health and safety policy pages
- Project gallery / portfolio
- Contact and enquiry form

This is not a ₦100,000 market. A well-presented oil services company will invest ₦500,000–₦2,000,000 in a website that helps them qualify for vendor lists.

**Typical project value**: ₦500,000 – ₦2,000,000

---

### 2. Hotels and Hospitality
Port Harcourt's hospitality market serves a constant flow of corporate travellers, oil workers on rotation, and business visitors. The difference between a fully booked hotel and a half-empty one is often just digital visibility.

Boutique hotels, serviced apartments, and event centres in GRA, Trans-Amadi, and the new Rumuola corridor are actively competing for corporate bookings — and most don't have websites that match the quality of their physical properties.

**Typical project value**: ₦350,000 – ₦800,000

---

### 3. Engineering and Construction Firms
Rivers State has one of the most active construction markets in Nigeria. Civil engineering firms, building contractors, infrastructure companies, and facility management businesses are all prime candidates.

Like oil service companies, these businesses benefit from professional web presence during procurement and tendering.

**Typical project value**: ₦400,000 – ₦1,000,000

---

### 4. Clinics and Private Hospitals
The growing middle class and expat community in Port Harcourt drives demand for quality private healthcare. Specialist clinics — cardiology, fertility, orthopaedics, dental — are opening regularly.

Most have strong patient loads through referral and word of mouth, but no professional online presence. A website that enables online booking and displays the doctor's qualifications significantly increases new patient conversion.

**Typical project value**: ₦300,000 – ₦600,000

---

### 5. Logistics and Haulage Companies
Port Harcourt is Nigeria's primary oil port and a major logistics hub. Transport companies, freight forwarders, marine logistics firms, and warehousing businesses have significant revenue but almost universally poor online presence.

**Typical project value**: ₦250,000 – ₦600,000
