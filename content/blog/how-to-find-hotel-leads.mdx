Hotels and guesthouses lose real revenue every day they rely solely on third-party booking platforms. A direct booking website — even a simple one — lets small hospitality businesses capture reservations without paying a commission on every stay.

---

## Why Hotels Are a High-Value Niche

- **Commission savings are a concrete number.** Every booking through a third-party platform costs 15-20% in commission — a direct-booking website is easy to justify against that math.
- **Visual content sells rooms.** Photos of rooms, amenities, and the property are exactly what a website is built to showcase.
- **Higher price point.** Hospitality businesses are used to spending on marketing and OTA commissions — a website is a comparatively small, obviously justified expense.
- **Ongoing update potential.** Seasonal rates, new amenities, and promotions create natural recurring work.

---

## Step 2: Prioritize No-Website Properties Listed Only on OTAs

A hotel that only appears on booking platforms (and not with its own site) is paying commission on every single booking with no alternative. That's the exact pain point your pitch solves.

---

## Step 3: Pitch Around Commission Savings

> **Subject: Save on booking commissions, [Owner Name]?**
>
> Hi [Owner Name],
>
> I noticed [Hotel/Guesthouse Name] doesn't have its own website — which means every booking goes through a platform that takes a commission.
>
> I build simple hotel websites with room photos, rates, and a direct booking or inquiry form — so guests can book with you directly, commission-free.
>
> Want to see a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI generates a personalized version of this pitch for every property in your results.

---

## Step 4: Design Around Photos and Direct Booking

When you follow up with a mockup, prioritize:

- A photo gallery of rooms and property amenities
- Clear room types and rates
- A direct booking or inquiry form (even a simple contact form is a strong first step)
- Guest reviews pulled from Google or OTA listings, if available

---

## Step 5: Price With Commission Savings as the Anchor

| Package | Includes |
|---|---|
| Guesthouse | 5–7 pages, room gallery, rates, inquiry form |
| Small hotel | 10+ pages, room types, amenities, direct booking form |
| Full hospitality site | + SEO setup, booking widget integration, seasonal update retainer |

Frame pricing against what the property already pays in OTA commissions — a website often pays for itself within a handful of direct bookings.
