Gyms and fitness studios sell memberships and class bookings — two things a website handles far better than a phone call or DM. Yet many independent gyms, studios, and personal trainers still operate with no website at all, relying entirely on Instagram and word of mouth.

---

## Why Gyms Are a Strong Niche

- **Membership sign-up is a natural website feature.** Class schedules, membership tiers, and sign-up forms are exactly what a website does best.
- **Visual content already exists.** Gyms have workout photos and videos ready to use — you're packaging content they already have.
- **Community-driven referrals.** Gym owners talk to other gym owners and trainers — a good result travels fast within the fitness community.
- **Recurring revenue mindset.** Gyms already think in memberships and subscriptions — a monthly website retainer fits their existing mental model.

---

## Step 1: Search Gyms and Studios by City

In [ProspectAI](https://prospectai.beamai.net), search "gym," "fitness studio," "yoga studio," or "personal trainer" for your target city. Each result is flagged for website presence.

Boutique studios (yoga, pilates, CrossFit-style gyms) are often better prospects than large chain gyms, which usually already have a corporate site.

---

## Step 2: Prioritize No-Website Studios and Trainers

A gym or trainer with no website is losing sign-ups to competitors who show class schedules and pricing online. That's the exact gap your pitch addresses.

---

## Step 3: Pitch Around Sign-Ups, Not Just a Website

> **Subject: Missing sign-ups, [Owner Name]?**
>
> Hi [Owner Name],
>
> I noticed [Gym/Studio Name] doesn't have a website — which means people checking out class schedules or membership pricing before joining have nowhere to look.
>
> I build simple fitness websites with class schedules, membership tiers, and a sign-up form — so new members can commit without ever calling you.
>
> Want to see a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI generates a personalized version of this cold email for every gym or studio in your results.

---

## Step 4: Design for Energy and Action

Fitness websites should feel high-energy. When you follow up with a mockup, prioritize:

- Bold photos or video of classes/training in action
- A clear membership pricing table
- A prominent "Book a class" or "Start free trial" call to action
- Instagram feed embed, since most fitness businesses are active there

---

## Step 5: Price With Membership Tiers in Mind

| Package | Includes |
|---|---|
| Trainer/solo studio | 5–7 pages, bio, schedule, booking/contact form |
| Gym/studio | 10+ pages, membership tiers, class schedule, sign-up form |
| Growth site | + SEO setup, Instagram integration, monthly promo updates |

Fitness businesses run frequent promotions and schedule changes — a strong candidate for an ongoing monthly update retainer.
