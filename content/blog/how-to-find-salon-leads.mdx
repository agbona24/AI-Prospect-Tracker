Salons, barbershops, and spas are a underrated web design niche. They rely heavily on repeat customers and word of mouth — but almost none of them have invested in a real website, even though booking, before/after photos, and service menus convert visitors into appointments.

---

## Why This Niche Converts Well

- **Visual proof matters.** Before/after photos and a gallery are exactly what a website does best — and exactly what salons need to show off.
- **Booking friction is a real pain point.** Most salons rely on phone calls or Instagram DMs to book appointments. A simple booking page solves a problem they feel daily.
- **High density per city.** Every neighborhood has multiple salons, barbershops, and spas — an easy list to build.
- **Low sales resistance.** Salon owners are used to spending on marketing (Instagram ads, flyers) — a website is a natural extension of that spend.

---

---

## Step 2: Target the No-Website Businesses First

Salons without a website are relying entirely on foot traffic, Instagram, and word of mouth. That's a real limitation they already feel — which makes your pitch land faster.

---

## Step 3: Pitch the Booking Problem, Not the Website

The strongest angle for this niche isn't "you need a website" — it's "make it easier for clients to book with you." Try:

> **Subject: Fewer missed calls for [Salon Name]?**
>
> Hi [Owner Name],
>
> I noticed [Salon Name] doesn't have a website — which means every booking depends on someone calling or DMing you directly.
>
> I build simple salon websites with online booking, your service menu, and a photo gallery — so clients can book you anytime, even after hours.
>
> Want a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI auto-generates a version of this for every salon or spa in your results list.

---

## Step 4: Show, Don't Tell

Salons are a visual business. Whenever possible, pull 2-3 photos from their Instagram or Google listing and drop them into a quick mockup before you follow up — it dramatically increases reply rates for this niche specifically.

---

## Step 5: Price With Add-Ons in Mind

| Package | Includes |
|---|---|
| Starter | Service menu, hours, location, contact form |
| Standard | + photo gallery, online booking widget |
| Growth | + Instagram feed embed, review widget, monthly content updates |

Booking integrations and photo galleries are easy upsells once the base site is live — salons keep adding new services and stylists, which means ongoing update work for you.
