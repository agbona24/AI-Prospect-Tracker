## The Anatomy of a Winning Nigerian Web Design Proposal

Before the templates, understand what every winning proposal does:

1. **Opens with the client's problem** — not your background
2. **Confirms your understanding** — shows you listened on the call
3. **Lays out the solution clearly** — no jargon
4. **Justifies the price with value** — not hours
5. **Makes next steps obvious** — one clear action
6. **Removes risk** — guarantee or revision policy

A proposal that does all six things will close 3–4x more often than a simple quote email.

## Closing Rate Tips

**1. Send within 24 hours of the discovery call.** The longer you wait, the colder the lead gets.

**2. Follow up if you don't hear back in 3 days.** A single follow-up message closes 20–30% more proposals.

**3. Call, don't just email.** Nigerian clients often decide on a call — not after reading a document.

**4. Show one portfolio example** with each proposal. Pick the one most similar to their business.

**5. Price confidently.** Hesitation signals uncertainty. State the price clearly and let it breathe.
