Clinics, dental practices, and veterinary offices are consistently underserved online — even though patients increasingly search for providers, check hours, and look for appointment booking before ever calling.

---

## Why Healthcare Businesses Are a Strong Niche

- **Patients research before booking.** People check reviews, hours, and services online before choosing a clinic — a missing website is a direct loss of new patients.
- **Trust is the whole sale.** A clean, professional website signals credibility in a category where trust drives the buying decision.
- **Appointment booking is a felt pain point.** Most small clinics still rely on phone-only booking, creating friction that a simple booking page removes.
- **Recurring relationship potential.** Clinics need ongoing updates (new services, staff, insurance info) — a strong foundation for retainer work.

---

## Step 1: Search Clinics by Type and City

In [ProspectAI](https://prospectai.beamai.net), search "clinic," "dentist," "veterinary clinic," or "medical center" for your target city. Each result is flagged for website presence.

---

## Step 2: Prioritize No-Website Clinics With Good Reviews

A clinic with strong reviews but no website is your ideal lead — they've already proven they deliver good service, they're just invisible to people searching online before those reviews are ever seen.

---

## Step 3: Pitch Around Patient Trust and Convenience

Healthcare pitches should focus on patient experience, not technical features:

> **Subject: A quick question about [Clinic Name]**
>
> Hi [Doctor/Owner Name],
>
> I noticed [Clinic Name] has great reviews but doesn't have a website yet — which means new patients searching online may not find you before they find a competitor.
>
> I build simple, trustworthy websites for clinics — with your services, hours, location, and an easy way for patients to request an appointment.
>
> Would you like to see a free mockup of what yours could look like?
>
> [Your Name]

ProspectAI generates a version of this personalized for every clinic in your results.

---

## Step 4: Highlight Accessibility and Compliance in the Design

When you follow up with a mockup, emphasize:

- Clear service listings (what conditions/procedures you treat)
- Insurance and payment information, if relevant
- A visible appointment-request form or click-to-call button
- Mobile-friendly design — most healthcare searches happen on phones

---
