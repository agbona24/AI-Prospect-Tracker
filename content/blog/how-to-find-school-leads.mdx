Parents research schools, tutors, and training centers online before enrolling their children or signing up themselves — but many private schools, tutoring centers, and driving schools still have no website, relying entirely on reputation and word of mouth.

---

## Why Education Businesses Are a Strong Niche

- **Trust and research go together.** Parents specifically look for information — curriculum, fees, facilities, reviews — before committing. No website means no information to find.
- **Enrollment cycles create urgency.** Schools have enrollment seasons, which means predictable timing for your outreach and follow-up.
- **Multiple decision points per year.** New term enrollment, holiday programs, and tutoring sign-ups all create fresh opportunities for updates and campaigns.
- **Referral-driven category.** A well-designed site for one school or tutoring center is easy to reference when pitching others in the same network.

---

## Step 2: Prioritize No-Website Schools and Centers

A school with no website is invisible to parents actively searching for enrollment information — and is likely losing prospective students to competitors with even a basic web presence.

---

ProspectAI generates a personalized version of this outreach for every school or center in your results.

---

## Step 4: Design for Parent Confidence

When you follow up with a mockup, prioritize:

- Clear program/curriculum information and fee structure
- Photos of facilities, classrooms, or staff
- Testimonials or reviews, if available
- A prominent enquiry or enrollment form

---

## Step 5: Price Around Enrollment Cycles

| Package | Includes |

Align your outreach and follow-up timing with enrollment seasons — schools are far more responsive right before a new term starts.
